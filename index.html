About School

The 'Beau Voyage' of Delhi Public School, Ranchi began on 1st July 1989 in the SAIL Township of Ranchi. The school aims at nurturing the innate potential of the students and mould them into passionate, committed, competent, sensitive, value based individuals with leadership qualities.
